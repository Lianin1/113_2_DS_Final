Google Sheet Survey Design Guide

To ensure the system can correctly analyze your data, please design your Google Sheet survey according to the following format:

Required Column Format

Column Description Format Requirements
A. Employee ID Unique identifier for each employee Text format
B. Gender Employee gender Male/Female
C. Department Department name Text format
D. Level Job level Junior/Mid/Senior
E. Work Environment Satisfaction 1-5 rating Number 1-5
F. Salary & Benefits Satisfaction 1-5 rating Number 1-5
G. Management System Satisfaction 1-5 rating Number 1-5
H. Work-Life Balance 1-5 rating Number 1-5

Qualitative Questions

After column I, you can add any qualitative questions, but at least one qualitative question is required. These questions will be used to generate in-depth analysis reports.

Usage Steps

  1. Design your Google Sheet survey according to the format above
  2. Send the survey to employees
  3. Copy the Google Sheet ID (from the URL)
  4. Enter the Sheet ID on the homepage and submit
  5. Wait for the system to process
  6. Click "Generate Analysis Report"
  7. Download the generated reports and charts
